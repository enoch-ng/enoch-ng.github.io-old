Arrangements

Arranging and transcribing music has been a hobby of mine for many years. I usually arrange music from video games and anime, though I'll end up arranging anything if I like it enough. Although most of my arrangements are probably too niche to be listed here, I have uploaded those that I think people might find useful.

  • Hacking to the Gate by Chiyomaru Shikura

    Instrumentation: Cello and Piano

    Tuturu! This arrangement of Hacking to the Gate, the opening song to the popular anime Steins;Gate, was originally a request from a friend who wanted to play it on his euphonium. Since then, it's undergone many revisions, but I've tried to keep the arrangement mostly loyal to the original song.

    Download: Score Cello Piano

  • Megalovania by Toby Fox

    Instrumentation: String Quartet

    This is probably the most well-known track from Undertale besides Death by Glamour. This one actually started out as an arrangement for alto and tenor saxophones, cello, and electric bass—quite the eclectic instrumentation. Fortunately, only minimal changes were needed to rewrite it for string quartet. In this arrangement, I tried to capture the energy and movement of the original as much as possible.

    Download: Score Violin I Violin II Viola Cello
