As a composer I'm constantly indecisive and can't figure out what kind of music I want to write. The result is that I use of variety of styles including rock, metal, orchestral, and video game music.

  • Thanatos Gambit

    2016

    I wanted this piece to follow the structure of a track that someone might listen to on its own, with some subtler soundtrack-like elements included. I opted not to use any synths this time, preferring to focus on balancing the heavy metal and orchestral aspects so that neither felt unimportant. I like this composition a lot, but I have no intention of finishing it.

  • Nothung

    2016

    When I started writing this piece, I wasn't exactly sure what kind of music I wanted to make - I had an idea and I just ran with it. Originally it was supposed to be more like a video game soundtrack, but about halfway in I wanted it to be a standalone piece, too. These ideas interacted and conflicted throughout the composition, and the result sounds to me like a curious but slightly confused fusion.
